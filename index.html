About Us

At Muwal Al Sham Flower, established in 1990, we have been guiding our guests on a culinary journey to the heart of authentic Levantine cuisine with over 35 years of experience. Our dishes are crafted with the finest ingredients and utmost care, reflecting the rich heritage and history of our beloved region. We are committed to providing a warm and welcoming atmosphere that embodies the true spirit of Damascus. Each visit promises a unique experience filled with delicious flavors and a cozy ambiance. Welcome to your second home!
